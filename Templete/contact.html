Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. Express delivery (2-3 business days) is available at checkout. Free delivery on orders above $200.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused items in original packaging. Simply initiate a return from your order history and follow the instructions.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also find it in your order history under "My Orders".
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Check checkout for exact rates.
How do I use a coupon code?
Enter your coupon code in the designated field during checkout and click "Apply". The discount will be reflected in your order total immediately.
