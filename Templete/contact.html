Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available for an additional fee. International shipping may take 10-14 business days.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $200. Use code WELCOME10 for 10% off your first order.
How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also view tracking information in your account under "My Orders."
Can I cancel or modify my order?
You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it, as long as it hasn't shipped yet. Contact our support team immediately for assistance.
